TO VERIFY SUPERPOSITION THEOREM

Instructions

  • STEP 1 : Set the values of resistances R1, R2, R3 by adjusting the sliders.

  • STEP 2 : Make connections as per the instructions given below:

  • CASE 1:(a) When we consider Voltage source and open circuit Current source.

  • (b)Connect voltage source (17-19 and 18-20).

  • (c)Connect the Ammeters (1-11 and 2-12), (3-13 and 4-14), (5-15 and 6-16).

  • (d)Now, Check the connections by clicking on ‘CHECK’ button.

  • (i)If the connections are ‘Invalid’ click on corresponding label to remove the connection.

  • (ii)And if the connections are ‘Right Connections’ then follow the below steps.

  • (e)Turn ON the voltage source supply by clicking on the ‘POWER’ button and vary the voltage by moving the slider.

  • (f) Now, Click on ‘ADD’ button to add the readings in the observation table.

  • (g) Don’t Reset the experiment, Remove the connections (17-19 and 18-20) by clicking on the corresponding labels.

  • Case 2:(a) When we consider Current source and short circuit voltage source.

  • (b)Connect current source (7-9 and 8-10) and short (17- 18).

  • (c)Connect the Ammeters (1-11 and 2-12), (3-13 and 4-14), (5-15 and 6-16).

  • (d)Now, Check the connections by clicking on ‘CHECK’ button.

  • (i)If the connections are ‘Invalid’ click on corresponding label to remove the connection.

  • (ii)And if the connections are ‘Right Connections’ then follow the below steps.

  • (e)Now, Turn ON the current source supply by clicking on the ‘POWER’ button and vary the current by moving the slider.

  • (f) Now, Click on ‘ADD’ button to add the readings in the observation table.

  • (g) Don’t Reset the experiment, Remove the connections (17-18) by clicking on the corresponding labels.

  • CASE 3:(a) When we consider both Voltage and current source.

  • (b) Connect current source (7-9 and 8-10) & voltage source (17-19 and 18-20).

  • (c) Connect the Ammeters (1-11 and 2-12), (3-13 and 4-14 , (5-15 and 6-16).

  • (d)Now, Check the connections by clicking on ‘CHECK’ button.

  • (i) If the connections are ‘Invalid’ click on corresponding label to remove the connection.

  • (ii)And if the connections are ‘Right Connections’ then follow the below steps.

  • (e)Now, Turn ON both the power source by clicking on the ‘POWER’ button.

  • (f) Now, Click on ‘ADD’ button to add the readings in the observation table.

  • STEP 3: Click on ‘CALCULATE’ button to calculate the total current.

  • STEP 4: In Calculation section, We have to manually calculate the total current by using the formula and verify it with the help of ‘VERIFY’ button.

  • STEP 5: Click on ‘PRINT’ button to takeout the print of the webpage.

  • STEP 6: Click on ‘RESET’ button to reload the webpage.
